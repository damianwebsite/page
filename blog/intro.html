Introduction!

Thank you for checking out my blog. Starting a blog is rather old-school; after all, there are many alternatives for writing articles online (Substack, LinkedIn etc.).So why did I start this blog?

Nevertheless, having a self-hosted platform gives me more satisfaction:

  • Here I am free from platform guidelines
  • With a blog I can keep my content AI-free - on many other platforms my thoughts would be influenced by AI content that is published in abundance!
  • It's fun; self-hosting means also more creativity!

Hope you'll come by soon for more articles :) I will share the links online on LinkedIn and instagram too!
